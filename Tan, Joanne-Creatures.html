Creatures

  • The Chimera

    • Defeated by Bellerophon

    The Chimera is a creature with a lion's head, a snake as it's tail, and the head of a goat from its back.
  • Cerberus

    • Conquered by Hercules

    Cerberus is a creature that guards the gates of the Underworld for Hades. He is a three headed dog that is very ferocious.
  • The Sphinx

    • From the Legend of Oedipus

    The Sphinx has a head of a woman, a lion's body and has eagle wings. This creature appears in the Legend of Oedipus. Oeidipus comes across the Sphinx. To pass, the hero must answer a riddle.
    What is that which in the morning goeth upon four feet; upon two feet in the afternoon; and in the evening upon three?
  • Hydra

    • Defeated by Heracles

    The Hydra is a serpent like creature with six heads. It's speacial capabilitites allowed it to regrow any limb that was cut off. The Hydra is the second task (labour) in the tale of Heracles.
  • The Minotaur

    • Defeated by Theseus

    The Minotaur has the head of a bull and the body of a man. This creature dwells in the labyrinth, located under King Minos (of Crete) palace.
